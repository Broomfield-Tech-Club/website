About Us

Broomfield Tech Club is a vibrant community of doctors dedicated to enhancing their digital skills to address the most pressing healthcare challenges. Inspired by the pioneering spirit of the skunkworks philosophy, we embrace bold and innovative approaches to problem-solving. We welcome all doctors who share our passion for progress and innovation to join us.

We hold regular sessions on various topics, from basic digital skills to advanced programming. Join our Discord and mailing list to be kept in the loop.
